What is Open Sky Slam?

We are a community of creative individuals that provide a platform for artists all around the world. We believe that anyone and everyone should be given a chance to speak. We believe that every artist deserves an audience that is both encouraging and equally passionate about their art as the artists themselves.

What do you get

Sometimes the best kind of people are found when you’re not looking for them. We, at Open Sky, believe in the magic of people. There is literally every kind of magical person in the world and at every Open Sky event, you get to meet one of each kind. So come and meet someone new, what are you waiting for?
  1. Slammers (attendees) of every event are exposed to a set of carefully curated artists that are both passionate and good.
  2. A new and vibrant audience would mean building new contacts that can be both socially and commercially beneficial.
  3. We are essentially an organization for the community which means that we would be supporting a charity for the betterment of said community.
  4. A platform to showcase one’s skills and passions without the fear of being rebuked or judged.
