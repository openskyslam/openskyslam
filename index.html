What is Open Sky Slam?

Open Sky Slam is a stage that believes in converting every random space into a platform that attracts artist of all the forms. You can be a Poet, Musician, Singer, Dancer, Theater artist, Magician Or someone who loves all of these, Open Sky is the new genie in your life.

When does it happen?

Every Sunday in a month we hold a slam/workshop at some corner of your City decorating it with artists of all the forms .

What is Open Sky Slam?

We are a group of creative enthusiasts who take pride in being a hippie community. Every area, however big or small is the hideout we always look for. And trust us, it NEVER is the same.  Open Sky proudly announces our initiatives making way for themselves at Cities like Mumbai, Calicut, Pune, Hyderabad and also in Karachi (Pakistan), Gold Coast (Australia) with its roots in Bangalore.

How do I participate?

Every month a formal invite goes up on our social media channels inviting every artist. One can get in touch with the team and the humongous family of Open Sky will welcome you with all the cheer.
