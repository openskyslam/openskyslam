What is Open Sky Slam?

Open sky is a stage that believes in converting every random space into a platform for artists. You could be a poet, musician, singer, dancer, theatre artist OR someone who indulges in all of this. We will welcome you wholeheartedly.

When does it happen?

Every month, on a Sunday, we hold a slam/workshop at some corner of your city, decorating it with artists from all forms. Be it a terrace top, garden, orphanage, cafe or pub- we intend to turn it into a creative space.

Where can you hunt us?

We are a gypsy community, and we are proud of it too! Open sky proudly announces its initiatives in the cities of Mumbai, Calicut, Pune, Hyderabad (India), Karachi (Pakistan) and our new venture in Gold Coast (Australia). However our roots lie in Bangalore.

How do I participate?

Every month a formal invite goes up on our social media channels inviting every artist. You can get in touch with the team and the humongous family of Open Sky will welcome you with all the cheer.
