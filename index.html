What is Open Sky Slam?

We are a community of creative individuals that provide a platform for artists all around the world. We believe that anyone and everyone should be given a chance to speak. We believe that every artist deserves an audience that is both encouraging and equally passionate about their art as the artists themselves.

We hold events that welcome a variety of people that celebrate and encourage art in all it’s forms, be it – poetry, music, dance, beatboxing or magic.

What do you get

We try to discover the city we live in by holding events in different places every time.- café, an orphanage or an open stadium. It’s our promise, that by the end of it, you’ll have a story for every road and every turn.

Sometimes the best kind of people are found when you’re not looking for them. We, at Open Sky, believe in the magic of people. There is literally every kind of magical person in the world and at every Open Sky event, you get to meet one of each kind.

So come and meet someone new!
